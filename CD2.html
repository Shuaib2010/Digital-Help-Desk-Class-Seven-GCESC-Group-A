Code: CD2- Call Diversion

Solution and precaution to these:

• Disable call forwarding on your phone. This is the simplest and most effective way to prevent call diversion. To do this, go to your phone's settings and look for the "Call forwarding" or "Supplementary services" option. Once you find it, disable all call forwarding options.
• Use a call forwarding blocker. There are a number of call forwarding blocker apps available for both Android and iOS devices. These apps will monitor your phone for call forwarding activity and block any attempts to divert your calls.
• Contact your mobile carrier. If you are concerned about call diversion, you can contact your mobile carrier and ask them to disable call forwarding on your account. They may also be able to provide you with additional information about call forwarding and how to protect yourself from it.
• Implement multi-factor authentication for account access.
• Regularly review and monitor account activity for unusual patterns.
