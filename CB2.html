Code: CB2- Cyber Bullying

Solution and precaution to these:

• Use strong passwords and keep them private. Don't share your passwords with anyone, even your best friends.
• Be careful about what information you share online. Don't share your address, phone number, or email address with people you don't know well.
• Be aware of your privacy settings on social media and other websites. Make sure your information is only visible to the people you want to see it.
• Don't respond to cyberbullies. This will only encourage them. Instead, block them and report them to the website or app where the bullying is happening.
• Talk to a trusted adult if you are being cyberbullied. They can help you figure out how to deal with the situation.
