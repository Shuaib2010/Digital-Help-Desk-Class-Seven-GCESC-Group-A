Code: R1- Ransomware

Solution and precaution to these:

• Keep your software up to date. Software updates often include security patches that can help protect your systems from known vulnerabilities. Make sure to install updates as soon as they are available.
• Use strong passwords and enable multi-factor authentication. Strong passwords are at least 12 characters long and include a mix of upper and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Multi-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security to your accounts by requiring you to enter a code from your phone in addition to your password.
• Back up your data regularly. In the event of a ransomware attack, having a backup of your data will allow you to restore your systems without having to pay the ransom. Make sure to store your backups in a secure location, such as the cloud or an offsite location.
• Use security software. Security software, such as firewalls and antivirus programs, can help protect your systems from ransomware attacks. Make sure to keep your security software up to date and to run regular scans.
