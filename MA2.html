Code: MA2- Malware Attacks

Solution and precaution to these:

• Keep your software up to date. Software developers release updates to patch security vulnerabilities. By keeping your software up to date, you can make it more difficult for attackers to exploit these vulnerabilities.
• Use a strong antivirus program and keep it up to date. Antivirus programs can help to detect and remove malware. Be sure to keep your antivirus program up to date so that it can detect the latest malware threats.
• Be careful about what emails you open and what attachments you download. Phishing emails are a common way for attackers to spread malware. Be suspicious of any email from an unknown sender, especially if it contains an attachment. If you are not sure whether an email is legitimate, do not open it or download the attachment.
• Be careful about what websites you visit. Malicious websites can contain malware that can be downloaded to your computer when you visit the site. Avoid visiting websites that you do not trust.
• Use a strong password and enable two-factor authentication. Strong passwords are difficult for attackers to guess or crack. Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security to your account by requiring you to enter a code from your phone in addition to your password when you log in.
